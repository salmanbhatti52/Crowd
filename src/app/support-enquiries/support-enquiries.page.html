Common Issues & Solutions
Login Issues
If you're having trouble logging in, double-check your email and password for accuracy. You can reset your password via the "Forgot Password?" link if needed. Still having issues? Reach out to Support.
App Crashes or Freezes
If the app crashes or freezes, try restarting your device, updating the app to the latest version, or clearing the app cache. Reinstalling the app may also help if the problem persists.
Slow Loading Times
For improved performance, ensure you're connected to a stable internet source. Switching between Wi-Fi and mobile data can sometimes resolve loading delays, as can closing other apps running in the background.
Notifications Not Working
To receive notifications, enable push notifications for the app in your device's settings. Verify within the app that notifications are turned on as well to avoid missed updates.
Update & Compatibility
Keep the app and your device's operating system updated for optimal compatibility. Check the App Store or Google Play regularly to stay current with new features and fixes.
